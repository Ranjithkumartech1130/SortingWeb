🫧 Bubble Sort

🫧 Imagine you have toys in a line, and you want the biggest toy at the end! Bubble Sort looks at two toys next to each other. If the left toy is bigger, they switch places! It keeps doing this until all the big toys "bubble up" to the right side, just like bubbles floating up in water! 🛁

🎯 How to Play the Bubble Game

1. 👀 Look at the first two toys side by side
2. 🔄 If the left toy is bigger, make them switch places!
3. 👉 Move to the next pair of toys and do the same thing
4. 🎉 After one round, the biggest toy is now at the end!
5. 🔁 Keep playing until all toys are in the right order!

🖍️ How to Organize Your Crayons

👨‍🏫 How to Organize Kids for a Photo

🔀 Merge Sort

🔀 Think about cleaning up two messy toy boxes! First, you split all the toys into smaller piles. Then you organize each small pile. Finally, you carefully put all the organized piles back together into one big, perfectly organized toy box! It's like magic! ✨🧸

💾 Space Complexity

🧸 How to Organize Your Toy Box

1. 📦 Split your big pile of toys into two smaller piles
2. ✂️ Keep splitting until each pile has just one toy
3. 🔄 Now start putting the piles back together
4. 👀 Look at two piles and pick the smaller toy first
5. 🎉 Keep doing this until all toys are back in one organized box!

🎯 Combine: [12, 25, 34, 64] | [11, 22, 88, 90] - Bigger organized piles!
✨ Magic: [11, 12, 22, 25, 34, 64, 88, 90] - One perfect toy box!
